# ☕ Monday Coffee Sales Analysis

## 📌 Objective

The goal of this project is to analyze the sales data of *Monday Coffee*, a company that has been selling its products online since January 2023.  

The primary objective is to recommend the **top three cities in India** for opening new coffee shop locations based on:
- Consumer demand  
- Sales performance  
- Market potential  

---

## ❓ Key Business Questions

### ☕ Coffee Consumption
- Estimate the number of coffee consumers in each city (assuming 25% of the population consumes coffee)

### 💰 Revenue Analysis
- Calculate total revenue generated from coffee sales in the last quarter of 2023  

### 📦 Product Performance
- Determine total units sold for each coffee product  
- Identify top 3 selling products in each city  

### 📊 Customer Analysis
- Number of unique customers in each city  
- Average sales per customer in each city  

### 🌆 City-Level Insights
- List cities with population and estimated coffee consumers  
- Compare **average sales vs average rent per customer**  

### 📈 Growth Analysis
- Calculate **monthly sales growth rate**  

### 🚀 Market Potential
- Identify top cities based on:
  - Total sales  
  - Total customers  
  - Estimated coffee consumers  
  - Rent efficiency  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

- SQL  


---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Significant variation in coffee consumption across cities  
- Certain cities show high revenue but also higher operational costs  
- Customer concentration plays a major role in profitability  
- Product demand differs regionally  

---

## 🏆 Final Recommendations

Based on data analysis, the **top 3 cities for new store openings** are:

### 🥇 Pune
- Highest total revenue  
- High average sales per customer  
- Very low average rent per customer  

---

### 🥈 Delhi
- Highest estimated coffee consumers (~7.7 million)  
- Strong customer base (68 customers)  
- Average rent per customer remains reasonable (~330)  

---

### 🥉 Jaipur
- Highest number of customers (69)  
- Very low average rent per customer (~156)  
- Strong average sales per customer (~11.6K)  

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

- Expand in **high-demand, low-rent cities** like Pune and Jaipur  
- Target **high population cities** like Delhi for long-term growth  
- Focus on **top-selling products region-wise**  
- Optimize pricing and promotions based on customer behavior
